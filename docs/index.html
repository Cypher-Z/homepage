I’m a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Haixin Duan. Currently, my research studies network (PKI, DNS and Cybercrime) security and cellular system (5G) security. I received my Doctoral degree in Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2022, and also received my Bachelor degree in Fundamental Science of Mathematics and Physics from Tsinghua Univeristy in 2017.
Research Interests: Network Security, Cellular System Security
